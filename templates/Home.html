Motivation for the project

We have often observed that doctors tend to ask for the patient and their family’s medical history. It is easy to remember small allergies but often forget or don’t fully understand the specifics of major diagnostics. To avoid any accident during any medical procedure, we created an archive where all diagnostics can be stored, making the job easier for both the patient and doctor.

About the project

Designed by the Grade 12 student, Dynamic Health is a patient friendly project. There are two parts to our project:

◉ Dynamic Health Model (in progress): a compact device utilising different sensors for general health check ups like temperature, oximeter, pulse etc.

◉ Dynamic Health Website: This website serves as display for the data received from the device. It also serves as an archive where users can upload their prescriptions under different categories to keep track of their health checkups.

Aspirations for the project

◉ We hope to keep working on this project and create a community where both doctors and patients are present, verifying doctors and digitising the process of prescription exchange.
◉ We hope to allow patients to create a sub community for their family so heads of the family can monitor health.
